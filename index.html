Full model details
01

Free assessment — we come to you

We evaluate your parking layout, electrical infrastructure, and tenant demand. You get a written proposal with honest recommendations — including if Enertech isn't the right fit. No cost, no commitment for this step.

02

We install everything — at zero cost

Enertech covers hardware, all electrical and conduit work, panel upgrades if needed, permits, and utility coordination. We navigate Xcel Energy rebates and Charge Ahead Colorado grants on your behalf. Timeline from agreement to live chargers: 3–4 months for most Front Range properties.

03

Chargers go live. Revenue arrives.

Tenants charge their EVs and pay Enertech directly via app or RFID card. Your property receives a monthly revenue share — typically the first payment within 30–60 days of launch. Tenants call Enertech when something's wrong. Your office is never involved.

Utility rebate

Xcel Energy EV rebate program

Xcel offers make-ready infrastructure and per-port equipment rebates for multifamily properties in their Colorado service territory. Pre-project application required before any installation — Enertech files it on your behalf.

State grant

Charge Ahead Colorado

A state-funded grant for EV charging at income-qualified and underserved multifamily properties. Can stack with Xcel rebates for qualifying properties. We assess eligibility and handle the application.
